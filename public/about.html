About Kelly's Closet

We’re four people from disparate backgrounds all brought together by a passion for technology and web development. Our paths through life all converged at one moment in time making Kelly’s Closet possible. A burning desire to become world-class coders lead the four of us to Rutgers Coding Bootcamp.

During our time together we’ve formed a strong appreciation for each other’s abilities, and our skill-sets fit perfectly together. Over the course of numerous projects together we naturally fell into our positions within the team. Alyssa and Kris on the front-end with an eye for UX/UI design, and Tim and Tom on the back-end handling everything from routes to databases. All of our hard work has culminated in the creation of Kelly’s Closet.
